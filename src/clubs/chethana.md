We're Chethana, the sports club of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru.

We are driven by the spirit of competition and the hunger to improve every single day. From intense practice sessions to high-stakes tournaments, we bring together individuals who thrive under pressure and play with purpose.

Here, every drop of sweat counts, every second matters, and every effort shapes a stronger athlete. We push limits, build resilience, and grow through every challenge we face.

Whether on the field, track, or court, Chethana stands for unity, effort, and the courage to give it everything.

We have an active and passionate community that constantly motivates each other to stay consistent, push harder, and grow stronger.

Testimonials include "Chethana turned my passion for sports into a lifestyle of discipline and confidence" and one person who shows up before sunrise, every single day, ready to train 🏃‍♂️.
