More than just a college club, ACE (Amrita Centre for Entrepreneurship) is a place where students develop their ideas and discover what it really means to start their own business. ACE is fundamentally motivated by entrepreneurship, which pushes students to think creatively and problem-solvingly rather than just theoretically. Our events are planned with this mentality in mind, emphasizing the identification of real-world issues, the development of workable solutions, and the comprehension of how concepts can be transformed into successful endeavors.
