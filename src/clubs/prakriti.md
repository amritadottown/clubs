We are Prakriti, the nature and environmental club of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru.

We are focused on fostering environmental stewardship, addressing the foundational elements of our ecosystem that every living being relies on. We forge the sustainable practices upon which our future depends, blending traditional conservation values with forward-thinking ecological solutions to tackle challenges like global warming.

Above all, we care about nature as our ultimate heritage. The environment holds intrinsic value in and of itself, serving as more than just a resource to be consumed. We approach sustainability with a mindset of practical innovation rather than passive observation.

We maintain an active community of dedicated individuals from Amrita and beyond. We regularly share practical insights on sustainable living and organize hands-on initiatives, such as eco challenges, to drive tangible impact. Testimonials include "This club fundamentally shifted my perspective on environmental responsibility" and members who consistently step up to lead local conservation campaigns.
