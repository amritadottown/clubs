Sankhya, where numbers inspire ideas and logic shapes innovation.

Sankhya is the Mathematics Club of Amrita School of Engineering, Bengaluru. We bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and the complex systems that power our world. Our mission is to uncover the fundamental mathematics, from linear algebra to stochastic processes, that drive modern technology like Spotify’s algorithms and Netflix’s recommendation engines. Through expert led talks, technical workshops (such as our MATLAB series), and  hackathons, we empower students to understand the "why" behind the tools we use every day.
