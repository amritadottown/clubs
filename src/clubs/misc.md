We’re MISC — the Microsoft Ignite Students Club of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bengaluru.

We’re a community of builders who turn a “quick workshop” into a late-night coding session. What begins as a simple workshop often evolves into intensive development sprints, where our members consistently push beyond the curriculum to master emerging tech stacks.

Our hackathons are high-energy. Our workshops are strictly hands-on. We don’t just passively follow tutorials, we deconstruct them, question them, and push the boundaries to see what we can truly create.

We consistently deliver a high volume of events, intensive workshops, and technical experiments, pouring our energy into making each initiative more impactful than the last.

From hackathons like Spooktacular Solutions to cybersecurity challenges like PrisonCTF, our philosophy is simple: learn by doing, failing, iterating, and proudly showcasing the solutions we build.

Above all, we are driven by a shared passion for technology and the shared excitement of solving complex problems together.
